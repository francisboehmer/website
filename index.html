What's GBDA?

GBDA stands for Global Business and Digital Arts. It’s program where we learn about interaction design, user experience design and many digital mediums that can leveraged to create great and meaningful products and services. This includes graphics design, video, audio and web design. We also learn about business and what makes these technologies profitable. The GBDA program also employs a focus on group work and project management, which prepares us for collaborative and team-based work environments.
