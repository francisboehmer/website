What's GBDA?

GBDA stands for Global Business and Digital Arts. It’s a program where we learn about interaction and user experience design and many digital mediums that can be leveraged to create intuitive and meaningful products and services. This includes graphics, video, audio and web design. We also learn about business and what makes these technologies profitable. The GBDA program focuses on group work and project management which prepares us for collaborative and team-based work environments.
