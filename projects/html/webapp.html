As part of a 24 hour hackathon named Starterhacks in 2018, three other team members and I designed and built an interactive prototype for an online service called Got It.

We began the design challenge by ideating to pin point an area of focus. We later determined that we wanted to address the challenge of recovering lost or found items.

What we created was an online forum named Got It for people to find and report missing personal belongings.
